Back to Papers

T Can you name how many things you could do with your phone. I bet you could name at least 50 tasks. That is the product of smartphone evolution which made many tools obsolete. One big thing that changed the dynamics of our society is the fact that now we have all the news of the world in our pocket and we can find out about an event on the other side of Earth within minutes. The nuance is that mainstream media companies have a business model where the main objective is not to keep the reader well informed but rather for them to constantly consume media. It is a “If you are not paying for the product, you are the product” situation. The main money is made by selling advertisements slots and pushing policies from government and corporate actors on to a large number of patrons. An unbiased news company does not exist. Even the most prestigious publications have their own interest at the end of the day, that is human nature. However it is evident that news which is behind a paywall tends to be better and of higher quality. That is probably why journals and newspapers still managed to survive in todays digital age, the printed information behind a paywall is of better value to people compared to the free news which exist to keep people in a perpetual state of stress and negativity as we have seen with the tribalization of political parties making it hard for people to think freely on each issues and making it convenient for them to pick a polar political side as their identity and discarding any room for thinking over details. If people come to the conclusion that the right way to go about news is to buy newspapers and journals we may get back the ritual of slower living and higher quality of content. Fast Mover Consumer Goods (FMCG’s) require a fast volatile network of promotional channels like social media ads. But as more people walk away from trendy lifestyles and consumption based living, value and quality will be factors which will hold higher decision value. Hence if you are an entrepreneur who is selling a high quality solution to a problem, it is well worth to consider advertising your activity on your local newspaper, as you will reach minds which prefer a slower information digestion.
